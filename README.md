# WorldwideRadioApp
A javaFX application I am currently working on, expected to be finished by December 2024

<h1>About this project<h1>
<hr>
<h5>This app contains 36 radio stations, across the world, from Ireland, England, USA, France, Germany and Japan.</h5>
<h5>Each country has 6 radio stations, made up of the most notable radio stations from each country.</h5>
<h5>The GUI is simple and easy to use, you click on an icon to start playing the station, and click om the country box at the top to switch countries.</h5>
<h5>There is also a toolbar at the bottom, with a play button, current station playing, and a volume control slider.</h5>
<h5>It also contains the current time aswell as the time in your selected country.</h5>
<hr>

<h5>I came up with the idea of this app as i am an avid languadge learner, and enjoy listening to radio stations in other countries.</h5>
<h5>I find radio in particular very interesting, I find it almost has a "peering into a different land" kind of aspect to it.</h5>
<h5>This is why I added the time in the selected country, to add to this kind of feeling i enjoy.</h5>
<h5>Personally I am Irish, so it was also important for me to have radio in countries that speak english, so I could understand them better.</h5>
<h5>I chose France, Germany and Japan as the non speaking countries mainly since i have some level of proficiency in speaking all 3, but i also think they are a good balance of countries to start on.</h5>
<h5>I am planning to eventually add China and Russia, if i can. Both these countries have some sort of blocking to western media, but I think they would be the most interesting to add.</h5>
